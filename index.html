Resource Rush – a simple Starknet idle game

Welcome to Resource Rush, a fully on-chain idle game built on Starknet using the Dojo game engine.

Players spawn a Miner that slowly generates Gems, claim their accumulated rewards and upgrade the Miner to increase production. The leaderboard tracks the total Gems claimed by each player.

How to play

  1. Connect your Starknet wallet.
  2. Spawn your Miner and start generating Gems.
  3. Claim your Gems periodically.
  4. Upgrade your Miner to increase your production rate.

This is a demonstration project. See the GitHub repository for the smart contract implementation and instructions.
